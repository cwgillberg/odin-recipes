Homemade sushi

Description

Always wanted to make sushi on your own, yet could never figure out how? Our simple yet delicious recipe will help you out.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed (Nori)
  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Your favourite toppings, like salmon, avocado, shrimp, cucumber, etc.

Steps

  1. Cook your rice: Depending on brand and manufacturer, cook your rice according to the recipe on the box, don't forget the rice vinegar!
  2. Spread out your nori (seaweed wrapper): Place them plat on the (dry) surface you are using for cooking
  3. Spread out your rice: Spread it out evenly and flatly over the surface of the nori, make sure to not forget the edges for an even experience
  4. Place your toppings on top of the rice: Spread them out so they cover the width of the nori
  5. Roll em up!: Preferrably using a bamboo mat, roll up the sushi rolls
  6. Chop em up!: Chop your rolls into even, neat pieces
  7. Enjoy!
