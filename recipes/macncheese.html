Mac n' Cheese

Description

Classic baked mac and cheese recipe. Just like your mom used to make!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning: salt and pepper, paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni: The simplest, but first step. Boil your macaroni according to the instructions provided by the container
  2. Make the cheese sauce: Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir
  3. Bake the mac and cheese: Bake in the oven until top is golden brown
